Our history


Archive photo

Difficult start

We started as a brick recycling company, a little later we realized that we were ready for more, and decided on the foundation of those bricks that we collected, we decided to build a garage, a kind of small studio where we could sit and discuss future plans

After a while, we began to engage in programming, and that radically changed our plans for the future, we decided to use our garage as a studio for the first time

And partly our name came from the city in which we lived, and so Lebedyn Studio was born
